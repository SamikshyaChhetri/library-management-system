Library Management System HELP Page
Welcome to the Library Management System! This guide will help you navigate and utilize the features of our app efficiently. If you have any further questions, feel free to reach out to our support team.
Getting Started
How can we help in Login and Registration
Register
Open the app and click on the "Register" button. Fill in your details including name, email, and password. Click "Submit" to create your account.
Login
If you already have an account, click on the "Login" button. Enter your email and password. Click "Login" to access your account.
What after Logging in
After logging in, you will be directed to the main dashboard. Here you can see an overview of the library's resources, recent activities, and quick access buttons.
